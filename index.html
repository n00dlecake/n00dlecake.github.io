Digital Designer

As an ambitious UX designer and avid consumer of digital content, I am keenly aware of the vast amount of material occupying our digital spaces. This awareness has led me to form a deep passion for the creation of meaningful content in an effort to contribute only impactful work online.

The featured portfolio piece is an application called Mahere Mahi Toi.

Mahere Mahi Toi

You canâ€™t travel far around the Otago Polytechnic without stumbling across one of the many art installations that populate our campus. Until now there has been no resource which allows people to navigate this carefully curated collection of artwork. Mahere Mahi Toi has been developed in collaboration with the Dunedin School of Art to address this opportunity.

As an application Mahere Mahi Toi features an interactive map, curated routes, and insights into the works and artists behind them, providing users with not only a wayfinding tool but also a deeper connection to the creative and cultural elements of our campus.
