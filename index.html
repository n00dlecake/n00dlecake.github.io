Digital Designer

Through the lens of an ambitious UX designer and avid consumer of digital content, I am keenly aware of the vast amount of material occupying our digital spaces. This awareness has led me to form a deep passion for the creation of meaningful content in an effort to contribute only impactful work online.

The feature portfolio piece is an application called Mahere Mahi Toi. This app includes an interactive map that enables users to locate and explore the Dunedin School of Art's collection of artworks installed around the Otago Polytechnic. Mahere Mahi Toi provides users with not only a wayfinding tool but also a deeper connection to the creative and cultural elements of our campus through curated routes and insights into the works and artists behind them. I aim to continue working with purposeful projects and have a strong desire to use my communication skills to uplift under-represented voices in digital spaces.
